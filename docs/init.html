Initializing a Selection

Edit this page

To initialize a selection, set the init property to a mapping or array of mappings from projected channels or field names to the initial value of the selection. Single selections are initialized with a mapping to single values, multi selections are initialized with a single mapping or an array of mappings to single values, and interval selections are initialized with a single mapping to arrays of values.
